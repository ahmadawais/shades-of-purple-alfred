## Easy Installation

1. Download [`Shades of Purple (with Border).alfredappearance`](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ahmadawais/shades-of-purple-alfred/master/Shades%20of%20Purple%20(with%20Border).alfredappearance) ‚Äî just click this link and Press <kbd>Cmd/Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>S</kbd> to save it as is on your system. Make sure the extension remains `.alfredappearance`
2. Double click to open it in Alfred, it will ask you to confirm the import. Do that.
3. Done!!! üôå

> You can also find [Shades of Purple on Alfred App's Site](https://www.alfredapp.com/extras/theme/F4biVVJlR3/).
